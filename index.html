About

You play as Steve “Shooty Shooty” Cooper. A former WW2 fighter pilot who according to legends and hearsay has the most confirmed takedowns in the entire US Air Force. When planet earth suddenly gets invaded by aliens, “Shooty Shooty” once again has to spread the wings of his steel stallion in order to save mankind from the extraterrestrial overlords.

Fast-paced shooting

A fast-paced game that tests players movement and shooting abilities. Easy to play but hard to master!

Customized Weaponry & Aircrafts

Select various weapons to use and type of aircraft to play as - all with different benefits!

Complete various missions

Complete various types of missions, destroying drones/aircrafts as well as the mothership to ultimately save the world from the alien invasion!

Gameplay

A demo of the game

Shooty Shooty Sky is a 2D Shoot em’ up game (SHMUP) where you take control of an airplane. The game is an homage to retro SHMUP games such as ‘1942’ and ‘Ikaruga’. The game is divided into levels, where the goal for each level will vary. Missions include killing enemy bosses, bomb radio towers, delivery etc.
